Our Story

WELCOME  to Punj aab's indian restaurant, where we strive to embody the spirit of Punjaab. Punjaab is famous for its food depicting the warmth and richness of it's heritage culture.

Our ingredients are sourced from quality purveyors featuring:

  • Fresh, natural, domestic lamb
  • Certified Beef
  • Free-range chicken
  • Sustainably harvested seafood
  • Locally sourced produce

Echoing the culinary traditions of Punjaab, our authentically original family recipes feature protein-rich legumes, earthy grains and exotic spices with a contemporary twist. 

We strive to bring to you Fresh & Simple Indian Food.
